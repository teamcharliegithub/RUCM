Information

This portal utilizes open-source intelligence (OSINT) resources to allow individuals to monitor the ongoing Russo-Ukraine conflict.

Sources

Twitter

All Tweets are pulled via publish.twitter.com and thus are sourced directly from authors via Twitter.

Asset Tracking

Flight and radar monitoring is implemented via <iframe> embed of flightradar24.com and maritime monitoring is accomplished via a similar embed of marinetraffic.com.

WebSDR

The WebSDR interface is accomplished via <iframe> embed of the WebSDR site of the University of Twente's Amateur Radio Club.
